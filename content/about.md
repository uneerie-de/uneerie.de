> **Uneerie**, *adj.*
> [ən′i:ri]
>
> Unaffected by fear, not apprehensive. See [Eerie](https://www.dsl.ac.uk/entry/snd/eerie), adj.
>
> Fif. 1864 W. D. Latto T. Bodkin xxxi.:
> *I wasna that uneerie mysel', when I heard the wark o' destruction gaun on.*
